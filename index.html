My Services

User Research

I conduct comprehensive user research to understand user needs, behaviors, and pain points. This involves techniques such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing to gather insights that inform design decisions. 

UI Design

I design visually appealing user interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. This includes selecting color schemes, typography, and layout to create a cohesive and engaging look that aligns with the brand identity.

IInformation Architecture

I organize and structure content in a way that makes it easy for users to navigate and find information. This involves creating sitemaps and user journey maps to visualize the flow of information.

Usability Testing

I conduct usability testing sessions to gather feedback on prototypes and designs. This iterative process helps identify areas for improvement and ensures that the final product meets user expectations.

Software Redesign

    I offer redesign services for existing applications or websites that may not effectively engage users. By evaluating current designs and understanding user needs, I create modern, appealing interfaces that enhance usability.

Consultation Services

I provide expert consultation on UI/UX best practices, helping businesses understand how to improve their digital products. This includes conducting audits and offering strategic recommendations for enhancing user experience.

Projects.

Peerfund

Seamless flows

PeerFund is a peer-to-peer lending platform that prioritizes user experience by making the journey of accessing loans straightforward and accessible. The design focuses on creating an intuitive user interface that guides both borrowers and lenders through the process with ease.The information architecture is structured to be easily understandable, featuring clear navigation and a logical layout that facilitates seamless flows between different sections of the platform. Key elements such as responsive design ensure compatibility across devices, while personalized dashboards provide users with quick access to their loan statuses and investment performance. By incorporating trust signals and feedback mechanisms, PeerFund fosters confidence and engagement, ultimately enhancing the overall user experience.

Digital Ducks 

Easy to navigate

Digital Ducks is an online store specializing in the sale of digital devices, designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for users. The platform features an intuitive interface that makes it easy to navigate through various product categories, allowing customers to quickly find and explore the devices they need.With user-friendly functionalities, shoppers can effortlessly add items to their cart, streamlining the purchasing process. This efficient design has contributed to a significant increase in sales, leading to a remarkable 50% boost in revenue. By prioritizing usability and accessibility, Digital Ducks ensures that customers enjoy a smooth and enjoyable online shopping experience.

Understanding users needs
